# Monetization & Canadian Setup

AdSense is the engine, but **watch-time-per-session is the real ambient money machine.** Lead with AdSense on long-form sleep loops where overnight autoplay stacks impressions; layer the automatic YouTube Premium line; include streaming royalties as one parallel line (see `references/audio.md`); then affiliates and later sponsors.

## YPP path & RPM levers

- **Requirements (verify current):** 1,000 subs + 4,000 valid public watch hours/12 months (OR 10M valid Shorts views/90 days). Early-access tier (fan funding only): 500 subs + 3,000 watch hours. Canada qualifies. Need no active strikes, 2-Step Verification, linked AdSense.
- **Split:** 55% to creator on long-form watch-page ads; 45% on Shorts (pooled). Premium follows 55/45 by watch time.
- **The inauthentic-content rejection risk is the dominant threat** — ambient/lo-fi/meditation have high YPP rejection rates (long continuous tracks + near-identical visuals). AI is explicitly allowed if "the substance of each video is materially varied." The review gate + original Higgsfield visuals + original hand-mixed beds are the mitigation.
- **RPM levers:** video length/mid-rolls (8+ min unlocks mid-rolls; can lift RPM 40–100%; place at natural breaks). **Ad config (validated):** mid-rolls **OFF** on overnight sleep loops (lean on Premium + session length); **ON sparingly** (1–2 manually placed) on shorter focus/pet content; **post-rolls OFF on sleep** (the most-complained-about ad event). Geography (US/UK/CA/AU high). Seasonality (Q4 +30–40%, Q1 −20–25%). Watch-time stacking (overnight autoplay; ambient AVD ~2–4h vs the ~7-min platform average multiplies effective per-view revenue).
- **RPM reconciled (VERIFY, wide variance):** ~$0.40–$4 for generic global ambient music; ~$10+ (one source: $10.92) for US-heavy sleep "soundscapes" with wellness advertisers. Blended ~$3–6 RPM (US-skewed): 100k monthly views ≈ $300–600; 1M ≈ $3,000–6,000; 10M ≈ $30,000–60,000. Ranges, not promises.
- **YouTube Premium line (underappreciated, automatic):** paid from the subscription pool by Premium watch time; background/screen-off play counts — exactly why a Premium subscriber leaving a sleep video on overnight banks hours. Typically 5–18% of income; overnight/background content with Tier-1 audiences can reach the top of/exceed that. A compounding co-engine — protect the back catalog.

## Secondary streams (ranked by ROI for faceless ambient)

1. **Streaming royalties** (already built in `references/audio.md` — one parallel passive line).
2. **Affiliates (low effort, modest):** sleep gear (weighted blankets, white-noise machines, mattresses, magnesium/melatonin), pet-calming (calming beds, anxiety wraps like Thundershirt, calming treats), audio gear. **Amazon Associates rates are low** (Pets/Home 3%, Health 1%, "all other" 4%, 24h cookie). **Passive sleep audiences are asleep and don't click** — concentrate links on shorter awake-audience focus/pet videos (descriptions + pinned comments). Higher-value: wellness *app* affiliates with recurring commissions (note **Calm has no public affiliate program**; competitors do).
3. **Sponsorships (higher dollars, harder for faceless AI, arrives late):** sleep/wellness/meditation apps + products (Headspace, BetterHelp, mattress, supplements) — advertiser demand exists ($16–20 CPMs) but a faceless AI channel is a harder sell (no host trust). Model: ~$500/deal at 5k subs, ~$1,500 at 25k, ~2×$2,500 at 100k. Direct outreach + marketplaces.
4. **Memberships / Super Thanks (minimal):** passive audiences rarely tip — enable at zero cost, don't model revenue.
5. **Music licensing — sync/stock (PARKED with the no-Suno decision):** sell music loops to stock (Pond5/AudioJungle ~50% splits); ~$200–500 year one. **Verify Suno's commercial/sync terms before external licensing.**
6. **Off-platform (Patreon/apps):** skip early.

**Effort-to-reward order:** AdSense > YouTube Premium (automatic) > streaming royalties (already built) > affiliates (low effort, modest) > sponsorships (high effort, scales late) > licensing (slow compound) > memberships (minimal) > apps/Patreon (skip early).

## Canadian tax / business setup (confirm with a Canadian accountant — not professional advice)

- **W-8BEN (do this FIRST, before monetizing):** submit in AdSense (Payments → Manage tax info). The Canada–US treaty caps US tax on YouTube copyright-royalty income at **0%** (Article 12). Use your SIN as foreign TIN. **Only US-viewer-sourced earnings are US-sourced.** Without a valid form, Google may apply **24–30% backup withholding across all earnings**, not just US ones. Form lasts through Dec 31 of the third year; re-file before expiry.
- **Income tax (T2125):** all income (AdSense, affiliate, sponsorship, memberships, streaming) is self-employment business income on Form T2125 → T1. No T4. Pay both employee + employer CPP. **Deductible:** Higgsfield/Suno/VPS + software (full in year paid), proportional home-office, equipment >$500 via CCA (half-year rule year one). **Foreign tax credit:** US tax withheld recovered via T2209 + provincial T2036 (dollar-for-dollar).
- **GST/HST:** register once worldwide taxable revenue exceeds **CA$30,000** over four consecutive quarters (or one quarter). AdSense is generally a **zero-rated export** (charge 0%) **but still counts toward the $30,000 threshold**. Canadian-brand sponsorships are fully taxable. Voluntary early registration lets you reclaim Input Tax Credits on Canadian purchases. Zero-rating is fact-specific — confirm with a pro.
- **Structure:** default to **sole proprietorship** early (cheap, simple; losses offset other income). Incorporate only when net income is consistently **~$80–100k+ AND** you can leave ~$20–50k inside the company (deferral at the small-business rate). Below that, incorporation's ~$4–10k/yr admin outweighs the benefit. Section 85 rollover later.
- **Other:** report worldwide income (CRA now receives platform data); convert USD→CAD consistently (Bank of Canada rate); keep receipts 6 years.

## The synthesized stack (sequenced)

1. **Pre-monetization (Day 1):** file W-8BEN; set up bookkeeping; distribute Suno music to streaming (parallel); build toward 1k subs / 4k hours with original differentiated uploads.
2. **YPP → AdSense (primary):** long sleep loops mid-rolls OFF; shorter focus/pet mid-rolls ON sparingly; title/SEO for US/UK/CA/AU; front-load Q4.
3. **YouTube Premium (automatic):** stacks immediately on long-form; protect the catalog.
4. **Affiliates (views steady):** sleep + pet products on awake-audience videos.
5. **Sponsorships (~25k–100k+ subs):** wellness/sleep-app/supplement/mattress brands.
6. **Licensing + memberships (opportunistic):** enable Supers/memberships at zero cost.
7. **GST/HST registration** when crossing CA$30k.

**Realistic mature-state (3-channel fleet, organic, ~$100 CAD/mo budget):** combined few-million monthly views → roughly **$3,000–10,000/month blended** (AdSense + automatic Premium + streaming + modest affiliate/sponsor), upside if one channel breaks out. Plan around the lower band.

**Thresholds:** cross CA$30k → register GST/HST. ~$80–100k net with retained earnings → model incorporation. A channel sustaining 1M+ monthly US-heavy views → pitch sponsors. RPM consistently <~$2 → audit geography + mid-roll settings on awake-audience content.
